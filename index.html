ABOUT ME

My name is Khalil Itto, and I am a 23-year-old content creator, university student, and multi-skilled professional from Marrakesh, Morocco. Since I launched my online presence in 2017, I have worked to establish myself as a helpful voice in the Arabic-speaking tech community, creating content specifically for beginners and students.

With three years of experience as an IT and English teacher, I bring a strong educational foundation to my work. This is most evident on my YouTube channel, "khalil droid," where I create detailed tutorials on a wide range of technical subjects. My videos cover everything from installing Linux operating systems and understanding cybersecurity concepts like firewalls, to providing practical advice for students, such as how to navigate local university online platforms.

In addition to my teaching and content creation, I am proficient in graphic design and have experience in the Print-on-Demand (POD) industry, which complements my online brand and merchandise store. My diverse skill set also extends to the Android development community, where I've been involved in projects related to the crDroid custom ROM.

Overall, I am a dynamic young professional who is passionate about leveraging my experience in education, IT, and design to create accessible and valuable technological content for my growing audience.
